Sources & References

APA Format 5+ sources required At least 1 non-website source

All sources below are cited in APA format. At minimum, five reputable sources are required, and at least one must be a non-website source — a peer-reviewed journal article, textbook, or book. Use EasyBib or CitationMachine to format your citations.

Citation Checklist

  • ○ At least 5 total sources
  • ○ At least 1 source is non-website (journal, book, or peer-reviewed article)
  • ○ All sources cited in APA or MLA format
  • ○ No source is a general encyclopedia (Wikipedia, etc.) as a primary source
  • ○ Sources are reputable (government, hospital, peer-reviewed, or professional organization)
